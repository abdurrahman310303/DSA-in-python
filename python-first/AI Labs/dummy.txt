once upon a time there was a bird who felt in love with a white rose one day
the bird came and proposed the white rose the white rose replied that i will
not love you until i become red, the bird went and after few days the bird 
came again and cut off its wings and drop all of its blood on the white 
rose. The white rose then become red, then the rose realized that how
much the bird loved it but it was too late because the bird had died.

Moral : Respect the feelings who loves you!
